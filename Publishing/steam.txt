[h1]Pyrinth Stove & Smithy[/h1]

Addon to Epochs - Pyrinth 

[h2]Features[/h2] 

[list]
    [*]Pyrinth Stove, a pyrinth-powered cooking stove. To cook food.
    [*]Pyrinth Smithy, a pyrinth-powered cooking smithy. To blacksmith stuff.
[/list]

That is it. Research pyrinth temperature regulation and built it.

They require fuel in form of pyrinth, but fairly rarely. You can turn fuel consumption off in mod settings.

[hr][/hr]
