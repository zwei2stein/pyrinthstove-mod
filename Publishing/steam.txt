[h1]Pyrinth Stove & Smithy[/h1]

Add-on to Epochs - Pyrinth

[img=https://i.imgur.com/XR8Qrfj.png]

[h2]Features[/h2]

[list]
    [*]Pyrinth Stove, a pyrinth-powered stove for cooking food.
    [*]Pyrinth Smithy, a pyrinth-powered smithy for blacksmithing.
    [*]Pyrinth Smelter, a pyrinth-powered smelter.
    [*]Pyrinth Crematorium, a pyrinth-powered crematorium.
[/list]

That's all. Research pyrinth temperature regulation (or pyrinth unleashing) and build them.

They require fuel in the form of pyrinth, but fairly rarely. You can turn fuel consumption off in mod settings.

[hr][/hr]

[h2]Mod Compatibility[/h2]

Recipes from mods are included by default.

[list]
    [*]More Linkables - support for linkables from this mod.
    [*]Dubs Bad Hygiene - pyrinth-powered boiler for water heating.
[/list]
